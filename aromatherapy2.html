Here are some key features of aromatherapy massage

At Calm Cove Massage, I invite you to escape the chaos of everyday life and discover a serene oasis of relaxation and rejuvenation. Our expert therapists are dedicated to providing personalized massage experiences tailored to your unique needs, helping you find balance, relief, and inner peace.

Essential oils

Aromatherapy massage uses highly concentrated plant extracts, known as essential oils, that are carefully selected for their therapeutic properties. These oils are derived from flowers, herbs, and other botanicals and are chosen based on their specific healing properties and aromatic qualities.

Customized Blends

At Calm Cove Massage, our skilled therapists will create a customized blend of essential oils tailored to your individual needs and preferences. Whether you're seeking relaxation, stress relief, or relief from specific ailments, we will select the perfect combination of oils to enhance your massage experience.

Inhalation and absorption

During an aromatherapy massage, the essential oils are applied to the skin through gentle massage techniques, allowing them to be absorbed into the bloodstream and penetrate deep into the tissues. In addition, the aromatic molecules of the oils are inhaled, providing therapeutic benefits through the olfactory system

Relaxation and stress relief

Aromatherapy massage promotes relaxation and stress relief by calming the nervous system and inducing a state of deep relaxation. The soothing scents of the essential oils help to alleviate tension, reduce anxiety, and promote a sense of well-being.

Physical and emotional healing

Aromatherapy massage can provide both physical and emotional healing benefits. The therapeutic properties of the essential oils help to alleviate muscle tension, reduce inflammation, and promote healing, while the aromatic scents can uplift the mood, balance emotions, and enhance overall mental well-being

Enhanced massage experience

Aromatherapy massage enhances the overall massage experience by adding a sensory dimension to the treatment. The pleasant aroma of the essential oils creates a calming and inviting atmosphere, making the massage more enjoyable and therapeutic

Health Benefits

In addition to promoting relaxation and stress relief, aromatherapy massage can provide a wide range of health benefits, including improved circulation, pain relief, immune support, and enhanced sleep quality.

Safe and Natural

Aromatherapy massage is a safe and natural treatment option that is suitable for most people. The essential oils used in aromatherapy are pure and natural, free from synthetic chemicals and additives, making them gentle yet effective for promoting health and well-being.
