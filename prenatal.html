PRENATAL MASSAGE

Experience the gentle care of our prenatal massage, designed specifically for expectant mothers. This soothing massage technique focuses on alleviating the common discomforts associated with pregnancy, such as back pain, swelling, and stress, promoting overall well-being for both mother and baby.

Here are some key features of prenatal massage

At Calm Cove Massage, we provide a nurturing and supportive environment for expectant mothers. Our expert therapists are trained in techniques that ensure comfort and safety during pregnancy, offering personalized massage experiences to help you feel relaxed and rejuvenated.

Reduced Swelling

Gentle massage techniques help reduce swelling in the legs and feet by improving circulation and lymphatic drainage.

Back Pain Relief

Prenatal massage focuses on relieving tension in the lower back, a common area of discomfort during pregnancy.

Improved Sleep

The relaxing effects of prenatal massage can improve sleep quality, helping expectant mothers rest better.

Reduced Anxiety

Massage therapy helps to lower stress and anxiety levels, promoting a sense of calm and well-being.

Enhanced Circulation

Improved blood flow helps deliver essential nutrients and oxygen to both mother and baby, supporting overall health.
